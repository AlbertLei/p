Fun Games

一些好玩的游戏:

  • The evolution of trust
  • Stable marriage problem (Adobe Flash required)
  • Banach–Mazur Game.
    • Let A be a subset of R. There are two players, Alice and Bob.
    • Round 1: Bob chooses a closed interval of positive length: U₁ ⊆ R. Then, Alice chooses a closed interval of positive length: V₁⊆ U₁.
    • Round 2: Bob chooses a closed interval of positive length: U₂ ⊆ V₁. Then, Alice chooses a closed interval of positive length: V₂⊆ U₂.
    • ... after countably-infinite many rounds, let E := ∩ᵢVᵢ. Alice wins if E ∩ A is empty. Otherwise, Bob wins.
    • If A is the set of rational number, will Alice have a winning strategy? What if A is an interval, say (a,b)?
